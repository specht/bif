# Example route to a strong ending

This is only one possible route.

1. Start in the Speisewagen.
2. Inspect the abandoned table and collect the powder.
3. Talk to Dr. Levin about Voss and the powder.
4. Search Voss's compartment and take the red fiber.
5. Search Adler's compartment and find the ticket and old photograph.
6. Inspect the luggage-car lock.
7. Promise conductor Falk not to accuse staff without proof; request the spare key.
8. Investigate Mara's stage case or gain her trust.
9. Trigger the midnight stop.
10. After the blackout, inspect the service area and identify Noll.
11. Confront Adler or Noll to learn the truth about the bridge.
12. Find the decoy case, then the real case in Mara's false bottom.
13. Open the documents.
14. Choose “Übergib Kopien an Staatsanwaltschaft und mehrere Zeitungen.”

This reaches **Die vollständige Wahrheit**. Other endings may be ethically defensible even when they reveal less.
